04 — The Framework

Nine Pillars. Both Channels.

Everything we do is designed to build visibility where it matters—in search engines and in the AI systems that depend on them.

01
Strong SEO Foundation
Rankings feed the retrieval pool that LLMs draw from. 52% of AI citations come from top-10 results. The foundation IS the distribution mechanism.
02
Structured Content
Formatted for both search engines and LLM extraction—clear headings, self-contained paragraphs, Q&A blocks, and tables that can be lifted cleanly into AI answers.
03
Deep Audience Research
We map your audience's questions, pain points, and search behavior in detail—so every page we build answers a real query or prompt they'd type into Google, ChatGPT, or Perplexity. Content mapped to actual audience intent performs in both channels.
04
Subject Matter Expert Content
30-minute sessions with clients extract real expertise no competitor can replicate. This feeds the E-E-A-T signals that both Google and LLMs prioritize—first-person authority, unique knowledge, and the kind of specificity AI-generated content can't produce.
05
Content Repurposing Engine
One 30-minute SME session becomes blog posts, long-form video, short-form clips, and text posts for LinkedIn, Facebook, and beyond. The same expertise appears across every platform LLMs monitor—multiplying your visibility from a single investment of time.
06
Expansive PR & Social
Builds both links AND brand mentions across platforms LLMs monitor—Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn, and news. When clients can lean into social, we prioritize it. Unlinked mentions carry more weight in AI than traditional SEO.
07
Aggregate Prompt & Fan-Out Analysis
We monitor how LLMs represent brands across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude—and analyze the sub-queries AI generates to map full topical coverage needs. These insights feed directly back into our SEO and content strategy.
08
Audience Channel Research
SparkToro-powered analysis of where audiences actually spend time—beyond Google. The answer to LLM visibility isn't LLM optimization. It's being everywhere your audience is.
09
Content Freshness
Ongoing optimization keeps content in the 2-year freshness window that AI systems prefer. 80%+ of AI-driven traffic goes to recently updated pages. This isn't just maintenance—it's actively feeding AI visibility.
